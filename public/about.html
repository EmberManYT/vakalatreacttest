How to use
Vakalat AI?

Vakalat Ai is a cutting-edge legal chatbot designed to provide accurate and specific information within the jurisdiction of Indian law. Whether you're seeking legal advice, exploring case precedents, or researching complex legal concepts, it's here to assist you.

The more precise your prompt, the better Vakalat Ai can tailor its response to your needs. Asking general questions will yield general advice, which may be unhelpful. Vakalat Ai will only outline options available to you, rather than guide you through them. It is not meant to be used inside a courtroom. Please read the disclaimer before using.

Vakalat Ai's attention span is currently limited to 5 messages per interaction to ensure optimal performance. It will forget any previous information given to it after that point, as it will enact a 'soft reset'.

About the AI
Technical information for geeks

  • The model it's built on

    Vakalat Ai is built on OpenAI's GPT 3.5 Turbo model. It uses chat completions endpoints to create a legal advisory experience. It possesses capabilities like message history (currently limited).

  • How it's trained

    Vakalat Ai is trained on information fed in chunks, that are vectorised and embedded. This is mainly done through OpenAI's Ada embedding model, but also utilises other technology stacks.

  • What it's trained on

    Vakalat Ai is trained on an ever-updating list of legal documents, which currently include The Constitution of India and the Indian Penal Code. View the full list of legal documents used here.

Disclaimer

Vakalat Ai is NOT a replacement for an actual lawyer, but merely a supplement, that may be used as an initial reference.
